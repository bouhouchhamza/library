Well wasted

Hayao Miyazaki

Author: Hayao Miyazaki

Genre: Biography / Memoir

Published: 2025

Language: English

Publisher: Studio Horizon

Description

Well Wasted: The Life of Hayao Miyazaki is a modern guide for anyone who wants to understand what it truly means to live a life guided by creativity, curiosity, and unwavering passion. Instead of following conventional paths or seeking only commercial success, this book explores how imagination, persistence, and dedication can transform not just your work, but your entire approach to life. Miyazaki’s journey shows that a life fully devoted to one’s vision—though often challenging—is never truly wasted. Through compelling stories from Miyazaki’s personal life, career milestones, and the creation of Studio Ghibli, the book demonstrates how to: Follow your passion even when the world doubts you, embracing risks and setbacks as part of the journey Blend art, nature, and humanity to create work that resonates across generations Balance perfectionism with practical progress, learning from mistakes and setbacks along the way Inspire others through leadership, vision, and the courage to remain true to your creative ideals Build resilience and patience, understanding that great work takes time and thoughtful care Leave a lasting legacy by turning personal passions into projects that touch the lives of millions Richly illustrated with anecdotes and insights, Well Wasted is more than a biography—it’s a roadmap for anyone who wants to create, inspire, and live with purpose.
